Safety First

Our Commitment to Your Safety

At Travel Buddy Finder, your safety is our top priority. We are committed to building a secure and trustworthy community where travelers can confidently connect, communicate, and explore the world together.

While we work hard to maintain a safe platform, it's essential for users to take personal precautions and use their best judgment when interacting with others.

Platform Safety Features

Verified Profiles
  • We offer profile verification through email, phone, and ID to help ensure you're connecting with real, trustworthy travelers.
Review System
  • After traveling together, users can leave reviews and ratings to promote accountability and transparency.
Report & Block
  • Easily report inappropriate behavior or block users you're uncomfortable with. Our team investigates all reports thoroughly.
Real-Time Chat
  • Communicate within the platform before deciding to meet. Never feel pressured to share personal contact details.
Safety Guidelines for Travelers

Before Connecting

  • Complete your profile and get verified.
  • Review profiles and ratings before accepting requests.
  • Use in-app messaging to build trust first.

When Communicating

  • Never share personal info like your home address or financial details.
  • Watch for red flags (evasive behavior, inconsistent stories, etc).
  • Keep conversations respectful and friendly.

Before & During Travel

  • Video call your travel buddy before meeting in person.
  • Share your plans with a friend/family member.
  • Meet in public first; avoid secluded locations.
