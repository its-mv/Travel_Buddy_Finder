What are cookies?

Cookies are small data files that are placed on your computer or mobile device...

Cookies set by the website owner are "first-party cookies"... others are "third-party cookies".

Types of cookies we use

Essential Cookies

These are strictly necessary to provide services and access secure areas.

  • Authentication cookies: identify users and preferences.
  • Security cookies: detect and prevent unauthorized logins.

Performance and Functionality Cookies

Used to enhance performance but not essential.

  • Preference cookies: remember settings like language.
  • Functionality cookies: remember choices in Service.

Analytics and Customization Cookies

Used in aggregate to understand usage or customize experience.

  • Google Analytics: analyze interaction.
  • Hotjar: understand user behavior.

Advertising Cookies

Make ads relevant, track conversions, and build audiences.

  • Facebook Pixel: ad performance tracking.
  • Google Ads: conversions and retargeting.
